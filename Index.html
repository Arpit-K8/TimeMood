About CLOCK-e-verse

CLOCK-e-verse turns plain time displays into interactive experiences. Every mode has a distinct personality, from calm and minimal to high-energy and playful.

Born from a "Study Game Challenge" between friends, this project transformed the simple act of time tracking into an engaging, mood-based experience. What started as setting timers for focused study sessions has evolved into a full universe of themed time experiences.

Built with passion by Arpit. Keep exploring, keep building.
